How InfuencedbyUS works

We believe that creating influencer marketing campaigns should be as easy as clicking a few buttons

Customize Campaign

You begin a custom campaign by uploading your media and relevant caption and hashtags. Then you select your preferred social media platform and input the appropriate caption and hashtags.

Find Infuencers

The next step is to find and filter influencers based on demographic information and their interests. You can limit the number of influencer and expected reach based on your budget.

Launch Campaign

All that's left for you to do is to pay for, and launch your custom influencer marketing campaign. All custom campaigns are reviewed by our agents before they go active.

Real Influencers, real results

Nano and Micro influencers have the highest engagement. Not just because of the strong connections with their audience, but also because of their niche and category-specific content. You can leverage this to reach your target audience.

Pay based on Reach

Pay for campaigns based on the reach of engaged influencers as opposed to the number of followers they have.
